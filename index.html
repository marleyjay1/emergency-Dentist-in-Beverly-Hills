What Qualifies as a Dental Emergency?

Not all dental issues can wait — and knowing the difference can save your smile and protect your long-term oral health. Contacting an Emergency Dentist in Beverly Hills is crucial for situations such as:

  • Intense tooth pain that doesn’t subside
  • Broken, cracked, or chipped teeth after injury
  • Knocked-out teeth needing urgent re-implantation
  • Loose or lost dental restorations
  • Severe bleeding or trauma to gums and soft tissues

These conditions don’t just cause discomfort — they can lead to lasting complications if not treated immediately.

How to Handle a Dental Emergency Before You Arrive

While immediate professional care is best, there are steps you can take while getting to the dentist:

  • Rinse your mouth with warm salt water
  • Apply cold compresses to reduce swelling
  • Use dental wax or sugar-free gum to cover sharp tooth edges
  • Keep a knocked-out tooth moist in milk or saline

These first-aid approaches can help protect your oral health until you reach the clinic.
